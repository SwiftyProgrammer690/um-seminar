UMMS LEAP Passion Project Archive

This is an archive of sixth, seventh, and eighth grade student presentations and artifacts. You are invited to watch and enjoy any presentation. If you reference any video, please cite it. Student presentations about summaries of their self-selected passion projects. This seminar course is an opportunity for middle school students to learn and share their passions for a subject and their passion for learning.

Topics:
Space - Games - History - Literacy & The Arts - Mathematics & Engineering - Science
